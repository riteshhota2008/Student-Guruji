Place of Opportunities
My rating:

At SRM Vadapalani, there is no limits to the opportunities, activities and fun a student can have. May it be culturals, guest lectures, industrial visits and so on. Granted, there are some unnecessary rules, which may seem a bit too strict to some(like 75% compulsory attendance), but the rules are there for a reason. Ragging is strictly prohibited here and anyone can register a complaint against any misbehaviour immediately. Which is a great plus point. Granted, some teachers are not up to the mark, and some way below the mark. Some teachers are exceptionally good, some need improvement in their vocational skills alone, and some need to start all over again.

Raghav Hari KrishnaSRM University - Vadapalani, Chennai

Amazing Experience
My rating:

Attending MES college has been a great experience for me. It is the 1st self financing engineering college in the state of Kerala. Campus has a wide network having a backbone of 1 GBPS. All the activities are automated including central library which consits of more than half lakh books, journals etc. The MESTECH tech fest hosted every year witness some extra ordinary talents. Even there are various things like IEEE, AECE, club activities & foremost college election which has a new experience in my academic life. And ragging is strictly prohibited but you will get little scary experiences.

It's a way of life

Manipal, the home of Manipal university, is almost exclusively made of students.
