About Us

We are a group of 4 engineering students who found it a chore to select one University out of the plethora of options avaliable. Not wanting other students to experience the problems faced by us, we created Student Guruji - A website made by Students solely for students. The website has every imaginable information about the top colleges in India and also reviews of the colleges given by the students currently studying. So expect no false advertising or exaggerated information about colleges in this website.

Our Vision

To have a collection of accurate information about the top colleges in India so that students don't get blinded by false advertisements and bloated features.
