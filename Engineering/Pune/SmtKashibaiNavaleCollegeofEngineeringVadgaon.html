Smt. Kashibai Navale College of Engineering, Vadgaon

Pune, Maharashtra | www.sinhgad.edu | Estd: 2001
Affiliated College | Contact : 020 24354938

About

Smt. Kashibai Navale College of Engineering is a technical institution in the city of Vadgaon Budruk, Pune, India. The institute is also known as SKNCOE. It is affiliated with the University of Pune, and managed by the Sinhgad Technical Education Society (STES). It has been accredited by the National Board of Accreditation and recognized by the AICTE. The institute has also been awarded an "A" Grade by The Directorate of Technical Education (DTE), Maharashtra state, Mumbai. The institute is located on the STES Vadgaon (Budruk) campus

Eligibility Criteria

  • B.Tech.

    • A pass in 10+2 or its equivalent.

  • For all programs: Minimum 70% aggregate in Mathematics, Physics & Chemistry.
  • For Bioinformatics, Biotechnology, Biomedical Engineering and Genetic Engineering: Minimum 70% aggregate in Mathematics / Biology, Physics and Chemistry.

Admission Procedure

  • Admissions are made through Maharashtra HSC marks and JEE Main Rank followed by counselling.
