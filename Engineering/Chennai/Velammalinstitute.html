Velammal Institute of Technology

Panchetti, Tamil Nadu | http://velammalitech.edu.in/ | Estd: 2008
State University | Contact : 044 27994065, 04427994066

About

Velammal Institute of Technology is an Institution of higher education meant for the aspiring youth, and comes under the direction of the Velammal Educational Trust. The College, established in the year 2008 and located in Ponneri near Chennai, inculcates a spirit of learning and discipline in a campus that offers top class facilities to its students. It ensures that an all round development is imparted to every student who enter the portals of the Velammal Institute of Technology, for sufficient focus is given to hone their communication and analytical skills. Spread in hundreds of acres of lush greenery the college hosts the best infrastructure, thus making the campus life both an educative and enjoyable one for the student. This sprawling VIT campus provides a holistic education in an ambience that makes no compromises on discipline, dedication and commitment. The result of which, students who pass out of the College turn out to be academically brilliant, morally upright and empowered individuals.

Eligibility Criteria

  • B.Tech.

  • Candidate must have completed 10+2 with PCM as subjects.
  • Candidate must have secured valid rank in TNEA.

Admission Procedure

  • Admissions are made through TNEA rank followed by counselling.
