Madras Institute of Technology, Chennai

Chennai, Tamil Nadu | www.mitindia.edu | Estd: 1949
Autonomous College | Contact : 04422516002

About

The Madras Institute of Technology (MIT) is an engineering institute located in Chromepet, Chennai (Madras), India.It is one of the four autonomous constituent colleges(University Departments) of Anna University It was established in 1949 by C. Rajam as the first self-financing engineering institute in the country, and later it merged with Anna University.

Eligibility Criteria

  • The Higher Secondary Examination of ( 12th) Curriculum (Academic Stream ) prescribed by the Government of Tamil Nadu with Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry as three or four subjects of the study prescribed under part III or any other examination of any university or authority accepted by the syndicate of the Anna University as equivalent thereto.

Admission Procedure

  • Admissions are made through TNEA rank followed by counselling.

Why Join Madras Institute of Technology?

  • "A" Grade accreditation by NAAC.
  • Deemed to be university under section 3 of UGC Act 1956.
  • Excellent placement record.
  • State of art infrastructure.
  • Scholarship and Loans provided.
  • Anti-Ragging University.
  • Ministry of HRD places SRM University in Category A.
  • Accredited by ABET for B.Tech courses.

Placements

  • The institute has an active Training and Placement cell which places its students in various organizations.
  • 70% of students take up coveted positions in corporates, 20% go overseas for higher studies in reputed institutions like MIT, Stanford, Carnegie Mellon, Cornell and Cambridge, and 10% turn into entrepreneurs.
  • Maximum Salary offered is INR 32 Lakh.
  • 4280 students placed in 265 companies.
