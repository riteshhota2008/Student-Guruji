Noida, Uttar Pradesh | www.amity.edu | Estd: 2003
Deemed University | Contact : 0120 2445252

About

Amity School of Engineering and Technology consists of seven departments namely Applied Sciences, Computer Science & Engineering, Information Technology, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Electronics & Electrical Engineering, Mechanical & Automation Engineering and Civil Engineering. It is one of the best engineering colleges in India. It is located in E-Block at Amity University, Noida Campus occupying an area one lakh sq. feet. The School has 220 faculty members, 100 support staff & over 4500 students. Our infrastructure is supported by 118 fully equipped laboratories. ASET offers more than thirty eight Programmes of which seven are the B.Tech Programmes, one B.Tech + M.Tech Dual Degree Program, four B.Tech + MBA Dual Degree Programmes, twenty six M.Tech Programmes. Ph.D Programmes are also offered in all subjects. (List of these programmes is given at courses offered option). Academic programmes at ASET are designed to enable growth and learning in a highly focused and application-based environment. This is achieved through a combination of formal lectures and hands-on experience in well equipped laboratories and through project activity.

Eligibility Criteria

  • B.Tech.

  • The minimum academic qualification for admission through JEE Mains is that the candidate must have passed in the final examination of 10+2 (Class XII), or its equivalent, referred to as the qualifying examination with 60% marks for general category candidates.
  • For Bioinformatics, Biotechnology, Biomedical Engineering and Genetic Engineering: Minimum 70% aggregate in Mathematics / Biology, Physics and Chemistry.

Admission Procedure

  • Admissions are made through JEE Mains rank followed by counselling.
