About

Ahmedabad University established the Institute of Information and Communication Technology /(IICT) in 2012. In 2014, with expanding programs in newer engineering disciplines, the Institute has assumed its current name-The Institute of Engineering & Technology (IET). IET delivers undergraduate and post graduate engineering programs with extensive student-centric pedagogies to produce excellent learning outcomes. Our project based educational approach creates dynamic and pro-active graduates with capacities for lifelong learning, complex problem solving, design and innovation and relating technology to society.
