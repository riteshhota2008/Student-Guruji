Sant Longowal Institute of Engineering and Technology

Longowal, Punjab | www.sliet.ac.in | Estd: 1985
Deemed University | Contact : 01672 280057

About

Sant Longowal Institute of Engineering and Technology (SLIET), a deemed university, was established by the government of India in 1989. The campus is situated on 451 acres in the Longowal, Sangrur district, Punjab, India. SLIET is fully funded by the Ministry of Human Resource Development, and is an autonomous body controlled by the SLIET Society. Educational opportunities include technical and practical training in the fields of engineering and technology.

Eligibility Criteria

  • A pass in 10+2 or its equivalent.
  • For all programs: Minimum 60% aggregate in Mathematics, Physics & Chemistry.

Admission Procedure

  • Admissions are made through JEE Mains.

Why Join Sant Longowal Institute?

  • 80-85% placements every year.
  • Scholarships for deserving students.
  • Deemed University by UGC.
  • Grade 'B' accreditation by NAAC.
  • Faculty consists of master's/Ph.D. degree holders.
  • Organizes industrial visits, educational tours and seminars.

Placements
