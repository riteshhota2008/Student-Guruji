Deemed University | Contact : 01824 404404

About

Lovely Professional University (LPU) is a private semi-residential university in North India created under the Punjab State Private University Act 2005 and recognized by UGC under Section 2(f) of UGC Act 1956. LPU offers around 200 programmes and courses at diploma, under-graduate, graduate, post graduate, and doctoral levels.The university has more than 25,000 students. Situated on National Highway-1, at the entrance to Jalandhar City, the university offers separate residential facilities for male and female students. It has connections with universities and institutes in the USA, UK, Australia, Canada, Singapore, Brazil, Poland, and Ghana for twinning programmes and student exchanges.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Pass with 70% aggregate marks in 10+2 or equivalent (with Physics, Mathematics and English) OR Pass with 60% aggregate marks in 10+2 or equivalent (with Physics, Mathematics and English) subject to valid score in LPUNEST-2016 / JEE (Main)/ State Level Entrance Test For B.Tech (Biotechnology) and B.Tech Biotechnology (Food Process Engg.) Subjects considered: Physics, Chemistry, English and Maths/ Biology/ Biotech, other things remaining same as above.

Admission Procedure

  • Admissions are made through JEE Mains or LPUNEST.
