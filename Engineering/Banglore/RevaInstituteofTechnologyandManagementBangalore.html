Bangalore, Karnataka |http://www.revainstitution.org | Estd: 2004
Affiliated College | Contact : +91-80-65687563

About

REVA Institute of Technology and Management was incepted in the year 2004 under the aegis of Rukumini Educational Charitable Trust as educational ventures of Divyasree developers. College is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belgaum and is approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi. Prominently located besides the road to international airport at Devanahalli, REVA ITM boasts of a sprawling campus of 35 acres full of greenery and an eco-friendly ambience. It has splendid building and state of the art facilities. The institute has a full fledged gymnasium and indoor game facilities like badminton court, table tennis, chess, carom etc. It has also has an air-conditional seminar hall with good audio-visual facilities. REVA ITM is proud of its state of art auditorium and amohitheatre with well designed acoustics and seating arrangements. The college also has excellent outdoor sports facilities like cricket ground, tennis court, basketball court etc with a spacious swimming pool in progress. high speed wireless networking, latest computer with legal softwares and dedicated internet connectivity, well-equipped laboratory, centrally located modern spacious library with rich collections of books, journals, browsing centre etc are few of the many feathers on the REVA cap. Audio visual centre with a capacity of 250 is always buzzing with activities. Various educational CD's have been procured in the library for the benefit of the staff and the students. A provision has been made to view the Eklavya technology channel and EDUSAT programs. Placement activities are given highest priority. All efforts are done to improve technical and soft skills through various development programs so that the students will be able to knock the door of the best companies in the industry.
