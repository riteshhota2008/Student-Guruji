Amity Business School

Noida, Uttar Pradesh | www.amity.edu/abs | Estd: 1994
Deemed University | Contact : 0120 4392669

About

Amity Business School is located in Sector 25, Noida.The institute was established in 1999. The major facilities at campus are library with over 1.25 lakh books & 17,000 online journals, auditoriums with a combined seating capacity of over 3,500, separate hostels for boys and girls, security through smart cards, biometric readers, latest IP cameras, fire warning systems, 24-hour guards and patrolling gypsys etc. The major recruiters at Amity Business School are Gillette India, Citi Bank, American Express etc. According to a survey conducted by Careers360, the institute is in the 36th position in global publication count. The major research publications are emotional intelligence and decision making etc.
