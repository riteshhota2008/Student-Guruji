Estd: 2012 | Deemed University | Contact : 0751 2432977

About

ITM School of Business, Gwalior is situated within ITM Universe Campus which is a prawling area of more than 125 on National Highway No. 75. The nearest metro station is Sithouli Railway Station that is located opposite to the campus. The major facilities at campus are central library with more than 400 international and national journals, an amphi-theatre with seating capacity of more than 3000 persons, separate libraries for each department etc.
