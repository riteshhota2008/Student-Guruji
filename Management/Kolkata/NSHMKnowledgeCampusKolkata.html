About

NSHM Knowledge Campus is in Kolkata.The institute was set up in 2002. The major recruiters are Kingfisher Aviation Training Academy, Trans World, Nicco Ventures, Aegis Global, ITC Wills Lifestyle etc. NSHM College of management & Technology acknowledges the fact that quality of technical and management education is becoming critical in today’s world. It, therefore as an integral part of NSHM Knowledge Campus, shall have pedagogy that weigh more towards creating and sharing domain knowledge wealth by way of rich academic partnership. The college offers Bachelor of Business Administration (Honours), Bachelor in Computer Application, Bachelor in Supply Chain Management, Bachelor in Hospital Management and also offers Masters in Hospital Administration courses.
