About

SP Jain Institute of Management and Research is located at Andheri West, Mumbai.The institute was established in 1981.The major facilities are library with more than 180000 volumes including the most recent publication in diverse areas of business, economics and management, 150 foreign and indian business journals and periodicals, audio -video cassettes and cd-roms , it centre, am phitheatre and executive training centre, separate hostel for boys and girls etc.The faculty of the institute undertake various research projects. The major research publications are experiential learning in management education, challenge with curricula in management education,a pan - india footprint of over-indebtedness of microfinance borrowers from and exploratory survey. The major recruiters are P and G, Colgate , Tata, HP, Standard Chatered etc.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum 3 years of Bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized University in India or abroad.

Admission Procedure

  • Admissions are made through CAT rank followed by counselling.

  • Students placed in leading companies.
  • Ranked 3rd in ‘Top 100 B-school Survey 2014’ by Business World.
  • Autonomous institute and AICTE approved.
  • AMBA accredited for PGPM, PGEMP, PGDM and PGFMB.
  • Member of Graduate Management Admission Council (GMAC).
  • Collaboration with foreign universities for student and faculty exchange programmes.
  • Partnership with IBM for research in Services Science and Management.
  • Promotes entrepreneurship among students.
  • Organises guest lectures, workshops, seminars and conferences.
